Our Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Crafted with Love

Ulhaan was born from a simple dream: to bring the authentic taste of traditional dairy and the healing power of organic herbs to every home.

From Our Farm to Your Table

Our journey began in the lush green pastures where our cattle graze freely, producing milk of exceptional quality. We follow time-honored methods passed down through generations to craft butter and ghee that taste like tradition itself.

With Mee Organics, we extended our commitment to purity into personal care. Using Ayurvedic herbs and natural ingredients, we created products that nourish both body and soul.

Every product carries our promise: pure, natural, and made with the same care we'd put into something for our own family.
